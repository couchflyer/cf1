Welcome from CouchFlyer family. This is about couchflyer, a diary of discoveries by a family of digital travelers. Seat back, relax as you join us travelling around the world, discovering the sights and sounds of places near and far. It is not just a trip planner but more of a destination diary. Explore the nuances of everyday life as experienced by the authors. Each page comes with a sense of belonging, an attachment to memories held dear by a family just like yours. Let us share our memories across, let us exchange the tips insights and leanings that m

ade us a bit more closer a bit more humane. This site is a collection of thoughts by three generations (see pic). You may find yourself relating to at least one of the family and in turn be one more of the family yourself.

Enjoy as you browse. Use these pages to plan your next vacation. Leave your comments. Connect in Facebook / Twitter / Google Plus / Pinterest for new updates. Join us. We would love to listen from you!
